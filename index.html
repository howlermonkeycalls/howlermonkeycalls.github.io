Howler Monkey Calls

Howler monkeys are common in many parts of Central and South America. Some of their calls are easy to hear at great distances and they fascinate many listeners.

This website presents all the calls that howler monkeys make.

You can listen to the calls on the mp3 file that is attached.

You can read the descriptions of the calls in the scientific paper that is attached as a pdf. That paper is titled: “Vocalizations of Howler Monkeys in Southwestern Panama” by John D. Baldwin and Janice I. Baldwin.

We hope you enjoy them.
